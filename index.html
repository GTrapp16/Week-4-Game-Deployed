The Ocarina of jQuery

Select either the Kokiri Emerald, Goron's Ruby, Zora's Sapphire, or the Ocarina.

By clicking on each symbol you will add a specific amount of points to your total.

You win the game by matching your total score to the target score.

If you go over the target score the princess Zelda dies and it is all your fault.

The value of each symbol will be added to your total score.

The hidden values will be reassigned for each symbol every game.
